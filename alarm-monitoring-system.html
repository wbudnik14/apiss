Alarm Monitoring System

Dedicated alarm system for use in many vessels, where control of automation and electronics systems is used on an ongoing basis.
The almost unlimited possibilities of our system are ensured by its heart, i.e. a programmable controller to which input and output signals are connected, both in analog and logical form. The system supervises the operation of assigned devices, and can also directly influence their operation through imposed procedures. A simple graphical and clear interface enables intuitive operation by many users. The graphic view can be personalized according to the customer's preferences. The systems we install are currently used on several large vessels and thanks to their many years of use, we are sure of their failure-free operation.
