Tunnel under Świna

Tunnel under Świna

In the second quarter of 2023, APISS took part in the electrical installation in the tunnel under Świna. We dealt with the execution of the fire protection system and the installation of heating cables on the facility.

Automatic Fire Extinguishing Devices

We have installed automatic fire extinguishing devices (SUG). Our scope was to connect each of the SUG stations and make a valve control cabinet. The installation required us to comply with fire safety standards and use fire-resistant materials. The central point of the installation is the Siemens Cerberus cabinet and control modules.

Fire pumps and heating cables

We dealt with the installation of cable tracks, cable routing, connection of two fire pumps and the pump control system. Our scope also included the execution of power supply cabinets and the installation of heating cables for pipelines.
