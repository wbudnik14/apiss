Dock deflection arrow measurement

Dock deflection arrow measurement system has been developed and implemented for Gryfia shipyard and approved by the Polish Register of Shipping.
Our system ensures the safety of the vessel while docking as well as logs and displays the following data:

  • the dock arrow deflection with 1 mm accuracy
  • the inclination of the dock with 1 mm accuracy
  • data are displayed on the control panel
